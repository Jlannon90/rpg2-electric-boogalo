Who are you?

Tinks

From the day she was born, Tinks has shared a deep connection with nature. The wilds have always been there for her in times of need - a vine snaring a foe that gets too close, and woodland creatures helping her to attack or escape. As a Druid, Tinks uses the power of nature to defeat her enemies. She's also 100% vegan, and she'll probably remind you of this multiple times. Her favorite food is Kale.

attack: Kale Fart

Ned

Ned has always been somewhat of a loner, and he told most people that was what he wanted. But deep down, Ned longed for friends and a sense of community - so he became a Necromancer. Making friends irl is hard, but summoned corpses never argue with you and they always have your back. Ned fights with his undead allies and dominion over darkness. His favorite food is fingers, any kind except chicken.

attack: Death Hug

Stunner

Stunner is a warrior with unique powers that came from being struck by lightning, and has a strong affinity for war paint. He literally colors himself in the yellow stuff everyday before leaving the house. Wielding electrical powers, his super charged attacks STUN enemies leaving them crippled before he strikes a finishing blow. He enjoys playing Pokemon and may or may not have a strange fetish for one of the characters. Stunner doesn't eat, he just plugs his tail into a socket.

attack: Electric Jazz Hands

Tex

Tex was drawn to the light after a near death experience at Mar del Taco, bit of advice, don't order the nachos. He joined the holy order as soon as he was able, and combined his affinity for delicious and spicy foods with the healing arts. You can buy his patented Holy Guacamole salve as seen on QVC. His favorite food used to be nachos, but now he sticks to burritos because they are softer and less likely to get wedged in your esophagus.

attack: Holy Guacamole
